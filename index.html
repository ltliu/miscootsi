Miscootsi is a vintage 1970s Piaggio Ape. Ape means "bee" in Italian — because these little trucks can be put to work! This Ape has been lovingly restored and converted to Electric. Having made the journey from Sicily to the Venice of America, Miscootsi is ready to scoot into your next event.

Miscootsi can be delivered to you, or pick up and rock up to your event. Outfitted with battery power and storage.

Based in Venice, LA.
